Case study: Developing a Digital Parenting Guide for Picky Eaters

Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive digital parenting guide designed to help parents of children aged 0–7 navigate picky eating with practical, evidence-informed strategies. The project combined structured research, AI-assisted content development, and user-focused writing to transform a common parenting challenge into an actionable digital resource.

Objective

To create a practical, easy-to-follow e-book that equips parents with realistic strategies, meal ideas, progress trackers, and confidence-building guidance for managing picky eating while positioning the product for digital publishing and passive income generation.

Approach

 Conducted research on child feeding practices, picky eating behaviors, and responsive feeding principles.
 Structured the content into logical chapters that guide readers from understanding picky eating to implementing practical solutions.
 Developed engaging, reader-friendly content using AI-assisted drafting and iterative editing to improve clarity and consistency.
 Included actionable resources such as meal plans, Nigerian-inspired recipe ideas, feeding trackers, and a 30-day challenge to encourage implementation rather than passive reading.
 Focused on creating a resource that balances educational value with real-life parenting experiences.

Metrics Tracked

 Completion of a structured multi-chapter manuscript.
 Development of actionable resources, including meal plans, recipes, and progress trackers.
 Readability and consistency across chapters.
 Alignment of content with the needs of the target audience (parents of children aged 0–7).
 Readiness for digital publishing and future monetization.

Outcome

Successfully produced a comprehensive digital parenting resource that goes beyond theory by providing practical tools parents can apply immediately. The project strengthened skills in AI-assisted content creation, long-form writing, research synthesis, project planning, and digital product development. It also established a reusable framework for creating future educational products in health, parenting, and personal development.
