Case Study

Developing a Digital Parenting Guide for Picky Eaters

Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive digital parenting guide designed to help parents of children aged 0-7 navigate picky eating with practical, evidence-informed strategies. The project combined structured research, AI-assisted content development, and user-focused writing to transform a common parenting challenge into an actionable digital resource.

Objective

To create a practical, easy-to-follow e-book that equips parents with realistic strategies, meal ideas, progress trackers, and confidence-building guidance for managing picky eating while positioning the product for digital publishing and passive income generation.

Approach

  • Conducted research on child feeding practices, picky eating behaviors, and responsive feeding principles.
  • Structured the content into logical chapters that guide readers from understanding picky eating to practical solutions.
  • Developed reader-friendly content using AI-assisted drafting and iterative editing for clarity and consistency.
  • Included meal plans, Nigerian-inspired recipe ideas, feeding trackers, and a 30-day challenge.
  • Focused on balancing educational value with real-life parenting experiences.

Metrics Tracked

  • Completion of a structured multi-chapter manuscript.
  • Development of actionable resources, including meal plans, recipes, and progress trackers.
  • Readability and consistency across chapters.
  • Alignment with the needs of parents of children aged 0-7.
  • Readiness for digital publishing and future monetization.

Outcome

Successfully produced a comprehensive digital parenting resource that goes beyond theory by providing practical tools parents can apply immediately. The project strengthened skills in AI-assisted content creation, long-form writing, research synthesis, project planning, and digital product development. It also established a reusable framework for future educational products in health, parenting, and personal development.
